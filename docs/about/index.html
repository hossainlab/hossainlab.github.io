Professional Overview

I am a biomedical researcher specializing in neuroepidemiology, cancer epidemiology, and cancer bioinformatics. My mission is to improve quality of life by operating at the intersection of education, technology, and health research. I currently serve as the Founder & Executive Director of CHIRAL Bangladesh, a non-profit biomedical research organization dedicated to addressing public health challenges in Bangladesh.

With a background in microbiology, my research portfolio spans the intersection of public health and machine learning, with applications across multiple quantitative sciences. I am passionate about leveraging data science and artificial intelligence to solve complex health problems and advance biomedical knowledge.

Teaching & Training

As a Lead Instructor at CHIRAL Training Unit, I design and deliver comprehensive biomedical data science training programs. Through hands-on learning and systematic approaches, I empower students and professionals to harness the potential of AI in healthcare applications. I also serve as a Health Data Analysis Instructor at cBLAST, University of Dhaka, contributing to the advancement of bioinformatics education.

Research Leadership

At CHIRAL Bangladesh, our research team investigates diverse topics in:

  • Big Data in Bioinformatics - Genomic and omics data analysis
  • Population Health - Epidemiological studies and health outcomes

I welcome collaborations with scientists, students, and research professionals interested in joint projects, research positions, thesis supervision, or training opportunities. Please contact me to discuss potential collaborations.

Core Research Areas

Our research focuses on advancing biomedical knowledge through computational approaches and epidemiological studies.

Primary Research Domains

Neuroepidemiology
Investigating the distribution and determinants of neurological disorders to understand risk factors and outcomes in populations. Focus on neurodegenerative diseases and neuroimaging applications.
Cancer Epidemiology
Studying patterns, causes, and control of cancer within populations to inform prevention and treatment strategies. Emphasis on population-based cancer surveillance and risk factor analysis.
Cancer Bioinformatics
Analyzing multi-omics data to identify genetic mutations, biomarkers, and develop data-driven approaches for cancer diagnosis and therapy. Integration of genomic, transcriptomic, and proteomic data.
Machine Learning in Healthcare
Applying advanced machine learning algorithms to healthcare data for predictive modeling, risk assessment, and treatment optimization. Development of clinical decision support systems.
Deep Learning Applications
Utilizing deep learning techniques to analyze complex datasets including medical imaging and genomic data for improved diagnostics and personalized medicine approaches.
