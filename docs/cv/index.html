Summary

I am Md. Jubayer Hossain. I am the founder and Executive Director of CHIRAL Bangladesh, a non-profit biomedical research organization dedicated to solving public health problems in Bangladesh. Additionally, I am also working as a health data analysis instructor at cBLAST. My professional endeavors aim to maximize the quality of life for individuals by operating at the intersection of education, technology, and health research.

I am interested in applying modern data science techniques in the context of disease diagnosis and large data analytics for public health while focusing on bridging the gap between computational and experimental laboratories through highly engaging and fruitful collaborations.
