About

I am a bioinformatician, researcher, and educator with over 5 years of experience in next-generation sequencing (NGS) data analysis, computational biology, and AI applications in biosciences. I have worked extensively with bulk RNA-seq, single-cell RNA-seq (scRNA-seq), spatial transcriptomics, and multi-omics integration across cancer genomics and infectious disease research.

My mission is to make bioinformatics accessible — whether you are a student starting from scratch, a wet-lab researcher wanting to analyse your own data, or a computational scientist looking to deepen your skills in machine learning and multi-omics.

What you can expect:

  • Personalised learning roadmap tailored to your background and goals
  • Hands-on project work using real biological datasets
  • In-depth guidance on NGS pipelines (RNA-seq, scRNA-seq, spatial omics)
  • Python & R programming for bioinformatics from the ground up
  • Machine learning applications in genomics and computational biology
  • Career advice: CV reviews, interview prep, and academic/industry pathways
  • Publication-quality figures and reproducible analysis workflows

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this mentorship for?
This programme is designed for students, wet-lab researchers, and early-career scientists who want to learn bioinformatics, computational biology, and data science. Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to advance specific skills, the sessions are fully personalised to your level and goals.
What happens in the first session?
The first session is a discovery call where we discuss your background, learning goals, current projects, and challenges. Based on this, I create a customised learning roadmap for you before our next meeting.
Do I need prior programming experience?
No prior programming experience is required. I teach Python and R from the basics for beginners. If you already have experience, we can skip ahead and focus on advanced topics like multi-omics, machine learning, or workflow automation.
How are sessions conducted?
All sessions are conducted online via Zoom or Google Meet. I share my screen, live-code with you, and provide Jupyter notebooks or R Markdown files after each session for your reference.
Can I cancel or pause my plan?
Yes, you can cancel at any time. I recommend completing at least one month to see meaningful progress, but there is no long-term commitment required.
